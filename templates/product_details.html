Product Description

The LED Surgical OT Light is designed to provide optimal illumination for surgical procedures. With advanced LED technology, this lighting system offers superior brightness, excellent color rendering, and minimal heat emission. The shadow-free design ensures that surgical sites are perfectly illuminated from all angles.

This surgical light features stepless intensity adjustment, allowing surgeons to customize the lighting according to their specific needs. The color temperature can be adjusted between 3500K and 5000K, providing flexibility for different types of procedures.

Built with high-quality materials and designed for easy maintenance, this OT light is an essential addition to any modern operation theater. The long LED lifespan of over 50,000 hours ensures years of reliable service with minimal maintenance requirements.
